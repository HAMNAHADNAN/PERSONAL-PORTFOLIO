CAR GAME

The car game was created using Python, utilizing a variety of its classes and modules. By calling different methods, we designed a 2D game where players control a car with the arrow keys, avoiding oncoming enemy vehicles. The car's speed increases every 5 points. When the player loses, they're asked if they want to play again.

3D DOT PRODUCT

This project, developed in Python, calculates the dot product of two vectors and displays it in a 3D space with x, y, and z-axes. It uses Matplotlib and Tkinter. Users input values for two three-dimensional vectors, and pressing the 'Dot Product' button computes the result. Additionally, there's a 'Plot' button for visualizing the product in a 3D graph.

MOVIE BOOKING SYSTEM

The C++ Movie Ticket Booking System, built with file handling, arrays, classes, and functions, simplifies ticket booking. It enables users to register, browse movies, reserve tickets, verify seat availability, and print tickets with important information. The system includes live seat tracking, up-to-date movie schedules, and an admin section for effective oversight of screenings, seating plans, and customer information.
